About
the Lab

RizqSpark is a deep tech AI research lab pushing the frontier of financial artificial intelligence. We build the data infrastructure, research foundations, and model alignment tools that the next generation of enterprise financial AI demands.

Our Mission

Aligning AI with the precision of financial expertise

Financial AI systems fail not because they lack intelligence, but because they lack the right data. General-purpose language models trained on internet text cannot reason about markets, earnings, and filings the way trained analysts do. RizqSpark exists to close that gap.

We do this by building proprietary, continuously collected datasets that capture not just what financial sentiment is — but how expert analysts reason toward it, where AI reasoning fails, and how those failures can be systematically corrected through targeted fine-tuning.

What We Are

A deep tech lab at the intersection of AI alignment and finance

RizqSpark operates at the intersection of three disciplines: financial NLP, reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), and enterprise AI deployment. Our work is grounded in published academic research and oriented toward commercial impact.

Our flagship project, SenseAI, is the first continuously collected, RLHF-aligned financial sentiment dataset in the literature — published on arXiv and submitted to leading AI conferences including IJCAI 2026 and CLEF 2026.

Commercial Applications

Where SenseAI
creates value

01 — Trading

Algorithmic Trading Signal Generation

Current LLMs systematically soften strong directional signals — classifying clearly bullish news as "Slightly Bullish." This hedging bias has direct position-sizing implications for algorithmic trading systems. SenseAI provides the correction data to calibrate models toward sharper, more accurate directional signals.

02 — Research

Earnings Call & Analyst Report Interpretation

LLMs exhibit forward projection and latent reasoning drift when interpreting earnings calls — injecting assumptions not present in the source text. SenseAI's reasoning chain data enables models to learn grounded, analyst-level interpretation that stays anchored to the actual document content.

03 — Compliance

Regulatory Filing Monitoring

Hedged corporate language in SEC filings, FCA disclosures, and EBA reports is precisely the register most frequently misclassified by general-purpose models. Fine-tuning on SenseAI data teaches models to detect and correctly interpret the nuanced sentiment signals embedded in regulatory language.

04 — Risk

Portfolio-Level Sentiment Scoring

Enterprise risk management requires consistent, calibrated sentiment scoring across large equity universes processed continuously. SenseAI's confidence calibration data corrects the miscalibration documented in our research — enabling reliable automated routing in high-throughput financial AI pipelines.

05 — Fine-Tuning

LLM Fine-Tuning & Model Alignment

SenseAI is architecturally aligned with RLHF training paradigms. Each data point provides a complete preference signal: the AI output, the human correction, the correction magnitude, the reasoning chain, and the market outcome. AI laboratories can integrate SenseAI directly into supervised fine-tuning and reward model training without additional preprocessing.

06 — Licensing

Proprietary Data Asset Licensing

SenseAI is available for licensing to AI laboratories, financial data providers, and institutional technology vendors. The combination of continuous collection, RLHF alignment, reasoning chain depth, and market outcome validation creates a dataset that cannot be readily replicated — both due to the domain expertise required and the time needed to build historical depth.
